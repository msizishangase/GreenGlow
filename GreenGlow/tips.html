Eco-Friendly Living Tips

Simple changes you can make to reduce your environmental impact

Sustainable Living Tips

Reduce & Recycle

  • Carry reusable shopping bags
  • Use a refillable water bottle
  • Separate your waste for recycling
  • Compost food scraps
  • Choose products with minimal packaging

Energy Saving

  • Switch to LED light bulbs
  • Unplug devices when not in use
  • Use natural light when possible
  • Wash clothes in cold water
  • Consider solar panels

Sustainable Eating

  • Reduce meat consumption
  • Buy local and seasonal produce
  • Grow your own herbs/vegetables
  • Avoid single-use plastics
  • Bring your own containers for takeout

Green Transportation

  • Walk or bike for short trips
  • Use public transportation
  • Carpool when possible
  • Consider an electric vehicle
  • Combine errands to reduce trips

Eco-Friendly Home

  • Use natural cleaning products
  • Install low-flow showerheads
  • Choose sustainable furniture
  • Use a programmable thermostat
  • Insulate your home properly

Sustainable Fashion

  • Buy second-hand clothing
  • Choose quality over quantity
  • Support ethical brands
  • Repair instead of replace
  • Donate or recycle old clothes
